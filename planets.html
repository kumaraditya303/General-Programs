Solar System.

The Sun, the eight planets with their satellites, and some other heavenly bodies, like asteroids, meteors and comets form the Solar System. The Sun is at the center of the Solar System.

The Sun

The Sun is the largest member of the Solar System. All planets, satellites, asteroids and comet revolve aroud it. IT is about 150 million kilometres away from the Earth. It produces a lot of heat and light energy which is essential for all living beings.


There are eight planets. They are as follows:-

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune
